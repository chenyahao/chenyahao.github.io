Welcome to my personal webpage! My name is Yahao Chen. I am currently a post-doc researcher in the group of [Systems, Control and Applied Analysis](https://www.rug.nl/staff/departments/11710) at [University of Groningen (the Netherlands)](https://www.rug.nl/) and my supervisor is [Stephan Trenn](https://stephantrenn.net/). I was a PhD student of [LMI](http://lmi.insa-rouen.fr/) at [INSA-Rouen (France)](https://www.insa-rouen.fr/) under the supervision of [Witold Respondek](http://lmi.insa-rouen.fr/9-membres/professeurs/19-respondek-witold.html).

Research Interests
======
* Linear and nonlinear differential-algebraic equations (DAEs)
* Nonlinear control systems
* Geometric control
* Switching DAEs with inconsistent initial values
